My Fav Receipe is Kaju Matar Paneer

The ingredients required
  • Paneer
  • Oil,Cumin
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Water
  • Gram masala
  • Fresh Cream
  • Coriander
  • Salt, Turmeric
  • Green and Red chilli
  • Tomato

    • The STEPS to prepare this receipe is as follows:

    1. Grind an onion, two tomatoes and cashew nuts in grinder and make a tomato paste.
    2. Heat oil in a kadai or a pan.
    3. Shallow-fry potato pieces on medium heat for 2-3 minutes.
    4. Drain and transfer to a plate.
    5. Now add jeera to same kadai and when they splutter add and sauté chopped onion until it becomes translucent.
    6. Add garlic-ginger-chilli paste and sauté until it loses the raw smell.
    7. Add tomato puree and mix well.
    8. Cook mixture for 3-4 minutes until the oil separates at the edges
    9. Add salt, dry fenugreek leaves, garam masala powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder and cumin powder and mix well.
    10. Add shallow fried potato, paneer and boiled green peas.
    11. Stir and cook for approx 1 minute.
    12. Add ½ cup water and bring mixture to boil over medium flame.
    13. When boils, reduce flame to low and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
    14. Turn off the heat and transfer prepared aloo matar paneer to a serving bowl and serve hot.
